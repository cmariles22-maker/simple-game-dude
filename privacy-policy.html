Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Carlos Mariles ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Simple Game Dude mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Simple Game Dude does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to use it.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores your high scores and unlocked levels locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. It is only used to save your game progress between sessions.

3. Audio

The App plays audio clips as part of gameplay. The App does not access your microphone, record audio, or collect any audio data from you.

4. Third-Party Services

Simple Game Dude does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data, the App is safe for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
